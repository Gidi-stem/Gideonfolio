Welcome to my academic record.

I founded the Educational Assess Development Initiative (EADI), its tutoring brand, Learn Academy and its STEM Club, to make maths, chemistry, and physics click for students who'd written themselves off. That same instinct for building systems that help people learn now shows up in the software I write: a clearance portal for schools, a STEM club platform, and the planner exhibited, which I use every week to run my own coursework.

When I'm not coding or teaching, I coach a football academy of over 40 young players and write. My two books explore academic motivation and record-keeping, which, funnily enough, is exactly what this site is for.
