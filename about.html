GOAL · 201

Career Aspirations

I want to keep building at the intersection of education and software tools that remove friction from how students prove their progress and how institutions verify it. CleP, my clearance portal, is the first real test of that: a digital signature workflow that could replace weeks of physical document chasing with a verifiable, sequential process.

Longer term, I'm aiming toward a role in EdTech product engineering, where I can pair classroom-tested instructional design with production-grade software and keep the Educational Assess Development Initiative growing alongside it.

  • 🎯 Ship CleP to at least one partner institution
  • 🎯 Grow the STEM Club into a repeatable curriculum
  • 🎯 Transition fully into software engineering practice
  • 🎯 Publish a third book on applied learning systems
